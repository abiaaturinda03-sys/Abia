Addressing the Forgery Crisis

Uganda has seen a surge in counterfeit academic documents. UNEB is committed to ensuring that only genuine UCE, UACE, and Primary Leaving certificates are used for employment.

Steps for Employers to Verify:

  1. Obtain a clear photocopy of the applicant's certificate.
  2. Identify the Year of Sitting and Index Number.
  3. Pay the verification fee via authorized mobile money channels.
  4. Submit the request through this digital portal or a regional office.
  5. Receive the official UNEB verification letter within 48 hours.

Verified Document Types

  • Primary Leaving Examination (PLE)
  • Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE)
  • Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE)
  • Technical & Vocational Certificates
  • Equating Foreign Qualifications
  • Confirmation of Results Letters

Public Announcement: Crackdown on Fraud

UNEB, in collaboration with the Uganda Police Force, has intensified its crackdown on printing hubs in Kampala suspected of forging academic documents. Several arrests have been made this month.

Employers are urged to verify all documents before finalising contracts to protect their organisational reputation and ensure competency in the workplace.
