Our Story

From the heart of Pachwa to your dinner table.

Excellence in Every Grain

A&A Distributors was founded with a single mission: to provide the highest quality maize flour to the people of Uganda. Based in Pachwa, Kagadi District, we take pride in our local roots and our commitment to "Super" grade standards.

Our maize is carefully selected, processed with modern techniques, and packed under strict hygienic conditions to ensure it is safe and nutritious for human consumption.

Our Mission

To lead the Ugandan maize industry by providing premium, energy-giving flour that supports the health of every household.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted distributor of maize products in East Africa, known for consistency and integrity.
